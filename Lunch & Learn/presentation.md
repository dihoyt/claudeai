### Common Questions:

**Q: How do I know if my prompt is good enough?**
A: Test it! If it gives you useful output 80% of the time, it's good.

**Q: Can I use these prompts commercially?**
A: Yes, they're templates. Customize for your needs.

**Q: What if the output is wrong?**

**Q: How do I handle sensitive data?**
A: Use placeholders in prompts, inject real data at runtime.
